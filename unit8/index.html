Espera para ODA

At the customs

Martha has just arrived in the United States. Now, she has to go through the customs, but she is not nervous, because she is prepared to answer the officer’s questions. Watch the video and do the exercise below.

Espera para vídeo

Customs Officer: Good morning! Welcome to the United States. May I see your passport and customs declaration form, please?

Martha: Good morning. Sure, here you go.

Customs Officer: Thank you. What is your final destination in the US?

Martha: I'll be staying in New York City.

Customs Officer: How long will you be staying in the US?

Martha: I'll be here for five days.

Customs Officer: And what's the purpose of your visit?

Martha: I'm here for vacation and to explore the city.

Customs Officer: Where will you be staying in New York City?

Martha: I have booked a hotel in Manhattan.

Customs Officer: How much currency are you carrying with you?

Martha: I have $500 in cash with me.

Customs Officer: Who packed your bags?

Martha: I packed them myself.

Customs Officer: Do you have anything to declare? Any gifts, souvenirs, or items for business?

Martha: No, I don't have anything to declare.

Customs Officer: Alright, everything seems to be in order. Enjoy your stay in the United States!

Martha: Thank you! Have a great day!

Practice it!
