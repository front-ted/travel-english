Cultural Differences

What customs do you usually associate with the following countries?

Espera para ODA

Japan

Removing shoes indoors

It is customary to remove shoes before entering someone's home, certain traditional accommodations, and some traditional restaurants. Slippers may be provided as an alternative.

The United States

Tipping

Tipping is a common practice in the U.S. It is customary to tip service industry workers such as waitstaff, bartenders, and taxi drivers. The standard tip is around 15-20% of the total bill.

England

Driving on the left

Traffic moves on the left side of the road in the UK. Pedestrians also follow a "look right" rule when crossing the road.

Brazil

Greeting with a kiss

Brazilians are generally warm and affectionate. It's common to greet friends and family with a kiss on the cheek.

The United Arab Emirates

Conservative dressing

Emiratis generally dress modestly, with women often wearing the abaya and men wearing the kandura. Modesty in dress is respected, especially in public places.

Mexico

D√≠a de los Muertos

Dia de los Muertos is a significant cultural celebration honoring deceased loved ones. Families create altars with offerings, visit cemeteries, and participate in parades.

Canada

Multiculturalism

Canada embraces multiculturalism, and cultural diversity is celebrated. Many Canadians take pride in their multicultural identity, and various cultural festivals and events are held throughout the country.

Spain

Siesta

The siesta, a short afternoon nap, is a longstanding tradition in Spain. Many businesses may close during the hottest part of the day, allowing people to rest before resuming activities.

Israel

Shabbat Observance

Shabbat, the Jewish Sabbath, is observed from Friday evening to Saturday evening. It is a time for rest, prayer, and family gatherings. Many businesses and public services close during this period.

Morocco

Islamic traditions

Islam plays a central role in Moroccan culture. Prayer times are observed, and during the holy month of Ramadan, fasting is practiced from sunrise to sunset.
