At a shoe store

Carlos’s second stop is at a shoe store called Trendy Shoes.

Listen to his conversation with a salesperson at the store and then do the exercise below.

Salesperson: Hello! Welcome to Trendy Shoes. How can I help you today?

Carlos: Hi! I'm looking for a pair of comfortable sneakers.

Salesperson: Great choice! We have a variety of sneakers in different styles and colors. What color are you interested in?

Carlos: I like black or blue sneakers.

Salesperson: Excellent! Let me show you our collection. Here are some black sneakers, and these are our blue ones. What size do you usually wear?

Carlos: I wear a size 7.

Salesperson: Alright, let me check if we have that size available in both colors. (Checks inventory) Yes, we have size 7 in black and blue. Would you like to try them on?

Carlos: Yes, please.

Carlos: These black sneakers feel comfortable. Can I also try the blue ones?

Salesperson: Of course! Here you go.

Carlos: These blue ones fit well too. I think I'll take both pairs.

Salesperson: Great choice! How would you like to pay? We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payments.

Carlos: I'll pay with my credit card.

Salesperson: Perfect! Please insert your card into the machine, and follow the instructions.

Salesperson: Thank you for shopping at Trendy Shoes! Here's your receipt, and your new sneakers.

Carlos: Thank you! They're very comfortable. Have a good day!

Salesperson: You're welcome! Enjoy your new shoes, and have a fantastic day!
